Biography

Professor Maddison [PhD (Sport and Exercise Science), MSc Hons, BHSc] is a Professor of Physical Activity and Disease Prevention, at Deakin University’s Institute for Physical Activity and Nutrition (IPAN). He leads the Digital Health Cross-Domain research theme at IPAN. Professor Maddison is a behavioural scientist with considerable expertise in the development and evaluation of digital interventions to support self-management of chronic diseases. His pioneering program of research has shown mobile health (mHealth) and digital interventions are (cost)effective modalities for improving lifestyle risk factors in the adults and young people, and for those with cardiovascular disease, and diabetes. He has published >250 peer-reviewed papers with >11,000 citations in Scopus (21,656 on Google Scholar), and procured totalling $41.2M ($11.2M CIA) in competitive funding—$16.89M in the past 5 years ($3.6 M as CIA). He has been named as an investigator on 18 internationally funded studies worth $7.7M. His research program has resulted in a step-change in digital health (including telerehabilitation) knowledge and positioned me as a leader (top 0.05%) of global telemedicine researchers (expertscape).
